🔍 How to Verify the Fix

If Forza Horizon 6 previously crashed with FH201 or FH205 and now launches successfully, the proxy DLL is working.

A log file may also be generated in the game folder after the DLL loads successfully.

⚠️ Troubleshooting FH201 and FH205

Still getting FH205?

FH205 may indicate that the driver is not reporting support for required DirectX 12 features such as Enhanced Barriers. Reinstall the AMD Work Graphs / Agility SDK driver and reboot your PC.

Game still crashing with FH201?

  • Verify your game files.
  • Remove other mods, overlays or third-party DLL files.
  • Delete conflicting files such as dxgi.dll, ReShade or OptiScaler files.
  • Test with a clean installation and only this d3d12.dll applied.
